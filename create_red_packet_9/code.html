Task Packet Guide

How it Works

Task Packets require users to complete specific actions (like following an account) before they can claim a share of the ETH. You can set tasks as required or as optional with a bonus incentive.

Best Practices

  • Provide clear, direct links. Double-check your links and handles.
  • Use public accounts. Our system needs to see public profiles to verify task completion.
  • Keep it simple. 1-2 tasks usually get the best engagement.

Maximizing Engagement

Share your LuckyPacket link widely on social media. A clear message like "Complete two quick tasks to claim free ETH!" can significantly boost participation.
