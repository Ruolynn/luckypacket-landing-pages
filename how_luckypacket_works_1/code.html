1. Connect Your Wallet

Securely connect your Web3 wallet, like Coinbase Wallet, and make sure it has some ETH on the Base network for transactions.

2. Create a LuckyPacket

Create a packet, set the amount, number of claims, and generate a unique shareable link for friends or communities.

3. Claim Your Luck

Open a shared link, connect your wallet, and claim a random amount from the packet, sent directly to your wallet.
