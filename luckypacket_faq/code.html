Help Center

How do I connect my wallet?

expand_more

To connect your wallet, tap the 'Connect Wallet' button on the homepage. You can then choose from supported wallets like Coinbase Wallet or MetaMask. Approve the connection request within your wallet app to start using LuckyPacket.

What are gas fees and why do I need to pay them?

expand_more

Gas fees are transaction costs on the Base blockchain. They compensate network validators for processing your actions, like creating or claiming a packet. Every on-chain action requires a gas fee paid in ETH.

Is my money safe in a LuckyPacket?

expand_more

Yes. When you create a packet, your funds are held securely in our audited smart contract. Funds are only distributed when claimed according to the rules you set. The contract is non-custodial, meaning we never have direct control over your funds.

What's the difference between a 'lucky' and 'equal' packet?

expand_more

In an 'Equal' packet, the total amount is divided equally among all claimants. In a 'Lucky' packet, each claimant receives a random amount from the total pool, making each claim a surprise!

Why did my transaction fail?

expand_more

Transactions can fail for several reasons, such as insufficient gas, network congestion, or if the packet has already been fully claimed. Check the transaction details on a block explorer for a specific error message.
